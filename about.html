About the test

Привет! I’m Darja — a linguist, a literary editor, and a certified Russian teacher. Yep. Not much more to say... Well, I put a lot of effort into making this test useful, objective, and a little bit fun, so hopefully it was worth it!

And hey, since you're reading this, let me just say that I sincerely admire your dedication to learning Russian. It really is a beautiful and interesting language!

This test is designed to give you an honest and precise picture of your current Russian level, from A1-beginner to C1-advanced.

It will also provide a full and detailed overview of your skills – your strengths and areas for improvement – that will show you exactly where to focus next.

A flexible system for scoring and results calculation – this is key to providing you with the most realistic understanding of your current proficiency level.

The test is structured into 3 main sections to check all key language skills:

'Section 1: Reading' (5 texts with question) will reveal your reading comprehension and vocabulary.

'Section 2: Listening' (3 audios with question) will test your ability to understand spoken Russian.

'Section 3: Grammar test' (20 questions)... well, you got the point, right?

The test typically takes 30-40 minutes. Take your time, but try not to get stuck on any one question, trust your gut – often, your first instinct is the best!

No, not really. But honey, there’s something that surely gets you one step closer to a certificate: a detailed report on your skills, showing your strengths and weaknesses, so your teacher (or yourself) can make the perfect learning strategy for you.
